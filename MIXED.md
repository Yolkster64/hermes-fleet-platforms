# Hermes Fleet: Mixed C++/C#/JS Variant

## Design
- C++ core, C# GUI, JavaScript for web/extension

## Pros
- Best performance, rich desktop/web UI, extensibility

## Cons
- Most complex integration, build, and maintenance

## Best for
- Advanced, extensible, high-performance systems

## Integration
- C++/CLI, P/Invoke, REST/gRPC, Electron, Blazor

## Comparison
- Most flexible, highest complexity
