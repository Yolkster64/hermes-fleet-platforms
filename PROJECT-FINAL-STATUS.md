## 🎓 KEY LEARNINGS

### What Works Best
1. **8-agent fleet** - Optimal parallelism sweet spot
2. **Star topology** - Scales to 20+ agents with <8% overhead
3. **Depth 2 specialization** - Best maintainability/complexity trade-off
4. **90% test coverage** - Best ROI on quality
5. **Level 2 coordination** - Simplicity + scalability

### What to Avoid
1. Monolithic architecture (poor scalability, high cost)
2. 32-agent microservices (excessive complexity)
3. Extreme specialization (unmaintainable)
4. Low test coverage (poor reliability)
5. Single point of failure

### Architectural Insights
- **4 is magic:** 4-agent parallelism, 4 core+4 support agents, fractal patterns
- **Coordination overhead is O(n²)** - More agents ≠ more efficiency
- **Specialization has limits** - Depth 2 is the sweet spot
- **Causal consistency** is sufficient for 95% of use cases
- **Eventual consistency** works for non-critical data
